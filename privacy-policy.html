Privacy Policy for Colores App

Last updated: March 29, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to the Colores app privacy policy. This policy explains how we handle your privacy when you use our educational app for children.

Information We Don't Collect

Our app is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from our users. Specifically, we do not:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Store any user data
  • Track user behavior
  • Use any analytics tools
  • Share any information with third parties

App Permissions

Our app requires minimal permissions to function:

  • Internet access: Required for loading app resources
  • Storage access: Required for saving user's drawings in the coloring minigame

Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for children and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy
